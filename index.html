FAQs

What types of businesses are eligible to use the application?
The application is open to both small and large scale businesses. The application is currently designed for the the businesses that sells physical/ tangible products.
How secure is the application?
The team is built on trust and we want that for our users. Trust is a key element of our existence. We follow best practices in data privacy and policy. Your information, data and funds are safe with us.
Is my money safe?
Definitely, your money is safe with the application. Be assured that until you receive your order and show satisfaction with your order, your money is still with us.
How will I know if my order has been dispatched?
You will get an email notifying you that the seller has dispatched your order and you will be expected to be available to receive it and submit a review within the agreed duration. You can also check the contract status to see if your order has been dispatched.
